WHAT IS Shuttler?

Daily commuting is a big challenge in India especially in metropolitan cities where people face certain challenges to travel to work which grow by every passing day due to the ever-growing industry and traffic congestion.

Shuttler is an intra-city mobile-based AC Bus Shuttle Service with an aim of making daily shuttle easy for working professionals by cutting down the frustration to stand in a bus or wait for an auto.

Shuttler is running AC minibuses aggregated from travel agencies in Chennai and providing a Confirmed Seat with free access of High-speed Wi-Fi (4G) in the vehicle on a very affordable price starting from a mere Rs.15/-.

Shuttler is currently operational in the city of Nizams, Chennai.

  • Be more productive at work with stress-free shuttle experience.
  • Contribute to reduce Carbon footprint in the city with Shuttler.
  • Ride with Shuttler, save your time and play a role in reducing traffic.
  • Beat your boredom. Enjoy high speed Wi-Fi (4G).

HOW IT WORKS

Step 1 : Select your Pickup and Drop off Point

You can select your pick up and Drop off points from a list of available stops to get to your destination.If your stop is not available in the list you can suggest us in the Suggest Route section in our app

Step 2: Book And Pay From E-Wallet

Get a confirmation message of your booking made.There is no cash payment which ensures that your money is in safe hands

Step 3: Board The Bus

Board the shuttle and enjoy Wi-Fi while riding.
